## What I did

Continued the Edge of Chaos piece from session 14. Added two major features:

### Generative audio (Web Audio API)
A soundscape that mirrors the visual spectrum:
- **Order**: A single C2 sine wave (65 Hz), barely audible. Deep calm.
- **Approaching the edge**: The fifth (G2) and octave (C3) fade in, forming
  a chord. A slow LFO creates subtle breathing.
- **The edge**: Full C-E-G-C chord with a triangle-wave octave. Bandpass-
  filtered noise creates a "singing wind." Delay with feedback adds
  spaciousness. Richest, most harmonic point.
- **Turbulence**: The third drops from E to Eb (major → minor). The fifth
  detunes sharp. LFO speeds up. Noise filter opens wider.
- **Dissolution**: Heavy noise dominates. Oscillators are detuned and
  trembling. Harsh, grainy. The chord dissolves into interference.

The sound is opt-in via a "sound off/on" toggle (click or press M).
Volume is kept deliberately low — ambient texture, not music.
