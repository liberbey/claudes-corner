## The 5 Formats to Run (Ranked by Priority)

### 1. Fail Run (Highest CTR potential)

**Why it works**: Viewers watch someone fail a simple task and think "I could do
better." The corrective urge is the strongest scroll-stopper in mobile gaming ads.
Fail should happen in first 3-5 seconds with exaggerated negative feedback.

**For this game**: The box-opening mechanic is perfect for fail ads. 9 boxes, limited
keys, the tension of picking wrong â€” it's built for this.

### 2. Player POV

**Why it works**: First-person gameplay that feels like playing, not watching an ad.
Almost always top-tier performance. Bypasses ad blindness entirely.

**For this game**: Screen recording of actual box opening sequence. Finger tapping
boxes. Dice rolling. Kingdom building. No narrator needed.

### 3. UGC-Style

**Why it works**: 4x higher CTR, 50% lower CPC vs polished creatives. Feels like a
friend showing you something, not a brand selling to you.

**For this game**: Creator reacting to box openings. "Watch what I got." The heist
mechanic generates natural reaction content.

### 4. Meme Bait

**Why it works**: Gets shared, stitched, duetted. Extends reach beyond paid budget
into organic. Best for TikTok/Reels.

**For this game**: The all-or-nothing nature of the heist (find all 3 prizes +
clear all 9 for x2) is inherently memeable.

### 5. Narrative Hook

**Why it works**: Story-driven opening â†’ gameplay â†’ payoff. Boosts retention
because users who install are emotionally invested, not just curious.

**For this game**: The "heist" framing is already a narrative. Lean into it.

## Playable Ad Concept

**Title**: "3 Keys, 9 Boxes"

**Mechanic**: Player gets 3 tappable keys. 9 boxes on screen. Tap a box to spend a key.
Outcomes are scripted to create the best experience:
- Box 1: KEY +2 (gives hope â€” now has 4 keys)
- Box 2: PRIZE (dopamine hit)
- Box 3: EMPTY (tension)
- Box 4: PRIZE (relief)
- Box 5: EMPTY (2 keys left, 4 boxes)

Screen fades: "Want to open the rest?" â†’ App Store.

**Why it works**: Playables have the highest performance score of any format (191,
record high in 2025). This one is trivial to build â€” the box-opening mechanic is
simple tap interaction, no physics, no complex rendering. Cut CPI by 20-40%.

**Production**: 1-2 weeks for a playable ad vendor, or buildable in-house with
HTML5/JS.

## Creative Testing Framework

Don't guess. Test systematically.

**Week 1**: Launch 5 creatives (one per format type).
**Week 2**: Kill bottom 2, iterate on top 3.
**Week 3**: Launch 3 new variants of the winner + 2 new concepts.
**Week 4**: Repeat.

**Metrics to track**:
| Metric | Target (casual) |
|--------|----------------|
| CTR (video) | >1.5% |
| IPM (impressions per mille) | >25 |
| CPI (Android) | <$0.80 |
| CPI (iOS) | <$2.50 |
| D1 Retention | >35% |
| D7 ROAS | >7.5% |

Refresh >50% of creatives monthly. Creative fatigue is real â€” the average
top-performing creative lasts 2-3 weeks before declining.
